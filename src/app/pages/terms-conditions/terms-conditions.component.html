2. Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you, automatically through your use of the Service, and through third-party integrations as Rzume evolves through its various phases.

A. Information You Provide Directly

This data is essential for the core functionality of the Service:

  • Account Data: Your email address (for sign-up, follow-up notifications, and the newsletter), and secure password or authentication details.
  • Job Application Tracking Data: Details of your job applications, including company names, job titles, links, and application status (applied, in progress, offered, rejected, etc.).
  • Document and Profile Data: Uploaded resumes and cover letters, and the associated scraped details and categorization.
  • Payment Data: Information necessary to process payments for the Add-On Credits and subscriptions.
  • User Goals: Application goals and streaks to drive consistency.

B. Information Collected Through Integrations

As Rzume rolls out advanced features, we will process data from connected third-party services:

  • Mail/Calendar Data: If you opt for integration, we access your email and calendar to automatically update application statuses (e.g., changing status to "In progress" upon receiving an interview invite or "Offered" upon receiving an offer letter) and set necessary events on your calendar.
  • Social/Professional Platform Data: If you opt for integration with LinkedIn, Glassdoor, or Indeed, we collect data to auto-populate your profile, interests, and automatically add jobs you applied to on those platforms to Rzume.
  • AI Feature Input/Output: Data you submit to utilize features such as resume optimization suggestions, tailored resume creation, AI-guided cover letter writing, and AI profile picture optimization.

C. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: Details about how you access and use the Service, including tracked applications, daily active users, and application streaks.
  • Device and Technical Data: Information about the device and browser you use to access Rzume (e.g., IP address, browser type, operating system).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide the Service: To operate the Core Job Application Tracker, manage application status, and provide Resume Management functionality.
  • To Communicate: To send automatic status updates, email reminders to follow up, and share the Newsletter and job tips.
  • To Enhance and Improve: To perform UI/UX Enhancements, develop and provide new AI Features, and deliver advanced analytics.
  • To Monetize: To manage the Freemium Model and process transactions for add-on credits and paid subscriptions.
  • To Connect Applicants and Recruiters: To enable Recruiters to recommend vacancies directly and access candidate profiles.

4. Disclosure and Sharing of Your Information

We share your information in the following circumstances:

A. Sharing with Third-Party Service Providers

We use necessary third-party service providers to help us run and maintain the Service. These providers are contractually obligated to keep the information confidential and secure. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Cloud Hosting
  • Web Browser Caching
  • Marketing Systems

B. Sharing with Recruiters and Hiring Managers

If you have an active Rzume profile, we will share your profile and associated documents with Recruiters and Hiring Managers for the purpose of job recommendations and recruitment. If you opt-in to the Hiring Manager Email Discovery feature, your profile may be used to enable decision-makers to be reached directly.

C. Legal and Compliance

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend our rights or property, or protect the personal safety of users of the Service or the public.

5. Data Security and Retention

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your password confidential.

Data Retention:

  • We retain your job application data as long as your account is active.
  • Applications that are set to "Rejected" will be moved to "Archive" after 30 days.
  • When a job is moved to "Archived," the added documents become disassociated from that application.
  • CVs/Documents cannot be deleted from your profile unless the job they are associated with is moved to "Archived".

6. Your Data Protection Rights (Nigeria)

As a data subject in Nigeria, under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), you have the right to:

  • Be informed: The right to be informed about the data we process (which this Policy aims to fulfill).
  • Access: The right to access and request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification: The right to request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): The right to request the deletion or removal of your personal data, provided there is no legal basis for us to continue processing it.
  • Object to Processing: The right to object to the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.

7. Policy Concerning Minors

The Rzume service is primarily intended for adults seeking jobs. However, the solution is also available to minors seeking legal employment. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from a child under the legal age for employment in Nigeria without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers immediately.
